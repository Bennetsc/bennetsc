Megalomania

May 2026

What's up with megalomania? Thinking of it from the brain's perspective, it's incredible how the brain will convince itself that it's superhuman simply because other people idolise them.

Perhaps AI would help advance the research for these conditions - on the psychological side - by allowing us to build mental models, feeding them with specific inputs, and then monitoring it's internal thoughts. We could essentially make the AI believe it's omnipotent, and then monitor its internal thoughts and 'J-space', as the Anthropic team call it.
