How to add a new essay

May 2026

This page is a template. To write a new essay, copy this file, rename it to something short with no spaces, and replace this text with your own.

The steps are simple. Duplicate this file. Rename it, for example to self-raised-adults.html. Change the title at the top and the title in the browser tab. Write your essay in place of these paragraphs. Then open the section page it belongs to and add one line pointing to your new file.

That line looks like this, and goes inside the list on the section page:

<li><a href="self-raised-adults.html">The adults who raised themselves</a></li>

One paragraph is one tag. Wrap each paragraph in its own pair of <p> tags and the spacing takes care of itself. Use <h2> for a section break inside a longer piece. Use the quote block above for anything you want set apart.

A note on the writing itself, since it matters more than the markup. Let one sentence carry one idea. Dense thinking wants to cram several ideas into a single breathless sentence, and the fix is almost always to break it apart and let each piece land on its own. That single habit does most of the work.
