Life philosophy

  • Be kind. Make somebody's day better. Treat them better than anybody has ever treated them before.
  • Be default happy. There is absolutely no upside in being miserable, and there is only gain in being happy.
  • Be useful. Do or make something that would improve the life of somebody, somewhere.
  • Be different. Stop copying the crowd. Introduce a new, unique way of experiencing life. It makes you special.
  • Think independently and reason into belief. Just because the herd is jumping off a cliff doesn't make it right.
  • Conform. It is important to be harmonious on a minimal foundation with society, or else society doesn't work.
  • Be curious. Be curious. Be curious. Being curious about everything widens your understanding exponentially.
  • Read! Read a fucking history book. You do not know anything about life unless you've read history books.
  • Focus. Focus on the important things in life. Focus on your goal. Limit what you pursue to just three goals.
  • Family. One of those goals should be your family. There exists no better experience in life than a loving family.
  • Purpose. Have a purpose in life. People with no purpose slowly wither away a miserable and useless existence.
  • Help. Always help anybody and everybody when you can. It is both self-fulfilling and improves a person's life.
